A little change can make a big difference

Giving Jar helps you discover #LoveLansing charities and makes it easy to donate to them when you shop online.

Try our free Shopper add-on for Chrome and sign up for our newsletter to learn about new charities.

Discover

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us, and read our blog to learn about Greater Lansing nonprofits and Giving Jar product updates.

Decide

Donations you make with Giving Jar will go to your charity of choice. Find the one that matches your passions with our charity research tools.

Donate

Shop any site online and when you're ready to check out, Giving Jar will ask if you want to make a donation. No obligations, just opportunities.
